Privacy Policy for Voice Guardian

Last Updated: February 2, 2026

1. Introduction

Voice Guardian ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Alexa skill and voice authentication service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Voice Authentication Data

  • Challenge-Response Patterns: We temporarily store challenge phrases and your responses for authentication purposes
  • Session Data: Alexa session IDs and timestamps for security and functionality
  • User Identifiers: Amazon user IDs to associate your Alexa account with your home automation system

2.2 Home Automation Data

  • Home Assistant Configuration: URLs and webhook IDs to connect to your smart home
  • Scene Triggers: Records of which scenes were activated and when
  • Authentication Attempts: Success/failure logs for security monitoring

2.3 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Skill invocations, intent requests, and error logs
  • Device Information: Alexa device IDs (provided by Amazon)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Voice Authentication: Verifying your identity through challenge-response verification
  • Home Automation Control: Triggering scenes in your Home Assistant installation
  • Security: Detecting and preventing unauthorized access attempts
  • Service Improvement: Analyzing usage patterns to enhance functionality
  • Technical Support: Troubleshooting issues and providing customer assistance

4. Data Storage and Retention

  • Active Challenges: Stored for 60 seconds, then automatically deleted
  • Failed Challenges: Retained for 24 hours for security analysis
  • User Account Data: Retained while your account is active
  • Logs: Retained for 90 days for debugging and security purposes

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell your personal information. We may share data only in these circumstances:

  • With Your Home Assistant: We send scene trigger commands to your configured Home Assistant instance
  • With Amazon: Through the Alexa Skills Kit for skill functionality
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, court order, or governmental regulation
  • Service Providers: With hosting providers (DigitalOcean) under strict confidentiality agreements

6. Your Data Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt-Out: Disable the skill at any time through the Alexa app
  • Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format

7. Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • HTTPS/TLS encryption for all data transmission
  • Encrypted database storage
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Automatic cleanup of expired challenges

8. Children's Privacy

Voice Guardian is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be stored and processed in the United States or other countries where our service providers operate. By using Voice Guardian, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries outside your residence.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the skill after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to know if personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Right to say no to the sale of personal information
  • Right to access and portability
  • Right to deletion
  • Right to non-discrimination

Note: We do NOT sell personal information.
